Mini Supermarket cashier application
------------------------------------
Datas:
------
1. product code
2. product name
3. quantity
4. membership discount

Mermbership type and its discount:
---------------------------------
1. Gold - 20% off
2. Silver - 10% off
3. Bronze - 5% off

Steps:
-----
1. Entering products
2. Calculating price
3. Calculating discount 
(with condition discount will be given only if billing amount exceeds $25 )
4. Making bill
